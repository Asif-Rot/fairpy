Fair Rent Division on a Budget

About

"Fair Rent Division on a Budget" by Procaccia, A., Velez, R., & Yu, D. (2018), https://doi.org/10.1609/aaai.v32i1.11465 .
The algorithm calculates Optimal envy-free allocation subject to budget constraints, or in simple words, calculates a fair rent division under budget constraints.
